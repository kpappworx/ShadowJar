Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many entries can I write for free? The free version allows one regret entry per day with one ritual animation.
  • Where is my data stored? All data is stored securely on your device. Nothing is stored in the cloud or shared.
  • Can I use Face ID to protect my entries? Yes, the archive can be locked with Face ID or Touch ID for extra privacy.
  • What happens when I delete a regret? You can choose to permanently delete it or archive it securely for future reflection.
  • How do I upgrade to Premium? Tap "Unlock Premium" in the app to enable unlimited entries, full rituals, ambient sounds, and archive access.
