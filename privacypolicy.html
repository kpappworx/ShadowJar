Privacy Policy – Shadow Jar

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

Shadow Jar is built with your privacy at its core. This policy outlines how your data is handled:

1. No Data Collection

Shadow Jar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All entries and preferences remain securely on your device.

2. On-Device Storage Only

Everything you write stays local. Shadow Jar uses on-device storage and never syncs with any cloud or external server.

3. Optional Biometric Protection

You can optionally secure your archive using Face ID or Touch ID. This data is not accessed or stored by Shadow Jar in any way.

4. No Tracking or Ads

Shadow Jar contains no ads, no tracking scripts, and no third-party SDKs. We don't monitor your usage or behavior — your reflections are yours alone.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to reach us at kpappworx@gmail.com.

By using Shadow Jar, you agree to this privacy policy.
